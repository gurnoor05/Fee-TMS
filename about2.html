About Us

Welcome to Test Hub, a platform for streamlining and managing online exams effortlessly. Our goal is to provide an efficient solution for educators and students alike.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower educational institutions with a test management platform that simplifies exams. We provide robust tools for creating, managing, and grading tests.

We support various formats, including multiple-choice, essay, and coding exams, adapting to diverse disciplines and certification requirements.

Meet the Team

We are a group of passionate first-year computer science students dedicated to improving online exams. With our expertise, we bridge the gap between technology and education.

Why Choose Us?

Our platform stands out with reliability, security, and ease of use. Features like real-time monitoring, automated grading, and anti-cheating tools ensure a smooth experience.

Future Plans

We are committed to evolving with the changing landscape of online education. Our roadmap includes incorporating AI-based analytics for performance insights, expanding support for more question types, and enhancing mobile accessibility. We aim to make our platform a comprehensive solution for all your test management needs.

Stay tuned for upcoming updates and new features that will make managing tests even easier.
